AI-Focused Learning Module

Usability Study — First-Year UX Students

Overview

Project Goal

The goal of this usability test is to determine how well first-year UX students engage with and learn from an AI-focused learning module. The study assesses whether students can understand how AI integrates into the UX design process, navigate the module efficiently, and interact with key content such as ethical principles and prompt-writing tasks.

This research builds on prior findings that revealed students struggle with understanding AI's role in UX, ethical usage guidelines, and writing effective prompts.
